About

East London's best record shop

We are an independent record shop founded in 2011 by Daniel and Rico ‐ school friends from East London with a mutual love for UK rap.

Growing up in the era where Grime was rife and popping, Dan and Rico would collect and burn CDs from local rappers. Their passion evolved with the scene and what started as a bedroom operation, trading mixtapes in school and the youth club, blossomed into something much bigger ‐ creating a community for sharing music.

“We want to stay true to our roots, and that is in the UK rap, the Grime, the Garage that we grew up on. We will forever support the underground UK scene, but when you check it nowadays, you'll see that the area's changing. It would be silly of us not to include a little Taylor Swift or some Metallica.” Rico

In 2018, Dan and Rico introduced the ‘Record of the week’ listening sessions ‐ a Friday evening ritual where music lovers gather from 6pm-7pm to discover and celebrate a standout record, handpicked by the team. Whether it's a freshly pressed R&B masterpiece or an obscure grime gem, the sessions are all about connecting over the love of music.

“We've been doing this thing for so long, it's just natural to us now. We got a great team behind us who love what we're doing just as much as we do. We've just got to keep the needle spinning”Dan
